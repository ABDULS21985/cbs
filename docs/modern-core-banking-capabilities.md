# 100 Capabilities of a Modern Core Banking Solution

> A comprehensive reference for evaluating, designing, or procuring a next-generation core banking platform.

---

## 1 · Customer & Account Management

| # | Capability | Description |
|---|-----------|-------------|
| 1 | **360° Customer View** | Unified profile aggregating all products, interactions, KYC data, risk scores, and transaction history into a single pane of glass. |
| 2 | **Multi-Entity Customer Onboarding** | Digital onboarding for individuals, sole proprietors, SMEs, corporates, trusts, and government entities with configurable workflows. |
| 3 | **eKYC & Digital Identity Verification** | Integration with national identity systems (NIN, BVN, NIMC), biometric verification, liveness detection, and document OCR. |
| 4 | **Flexible Account Structures** | Support for individual, joint, minor, trust, escrow, nostro/vostro, pooled, and omnibus account types with unlimited sub-accounts. |
| 5 | **Customer Segmentation Engine** | Rule-based and ML-driven segmentation by demographics, behaviour, profitability, risk tier, and product affinity. |
| 6 | **Relationship & Household Mapping** | Hierarchical modelling of parent-child, guarantor, beneficial owner, and corporate group relationships. |
| 7 | **Automated Account Lifecycle Management** | Configurable rules for dormancy detection, reactivation, escheatment, closure, and archival across all product types. |
| 8 | **Self-Service Customer Portal** | White-labelled web and mobile portal for balance enquiry, statement download, profile updates, dispute filing, and product applications. |
