# BIAN Service Domain Landscape (v13/v14 — ~322 Service Domains)

> Reference compiled from the official BIAN Service Landscape V13.0 Matrix View and V8.0 Overviews.
> BIAN organizes the banking landscape into **5 Business Areas**, **~36 Business Domains**, and **~322 Service Domains**.
> Version 14 (Feb 2026) rationalised some domains, added 3 insurance SDs, and restructured several payment SDs.

## V14.0 Notable Changes (Feb 2026)

- **3 new Insurance Service Domains** added (Insurance Policy Administration, Insurance Claims, Insurance Underwriting — names to be confirmed via portal)
- **Payment Area restructured**: some SDs renamed, removed, and added
- **Rationalization**: unnecessary service operations eliminated across all SDs

## Quick Reference: Core Banking Product Domains

For gap analysis against a core banking application, the most relevant service domains are:

### Deposits
- Current Account, Corporate Current Account, Savings Account, Term Deposit, Term Deposit Framework Agreement, Virtual Account, Standing Order

### Lending
- Consumer Loan, Corporate Loan, Mortgage Loan, Merchandising Loan, Loan, Leasing, Corporate Lease, Fiduciary Agreement

### Cards
- Credit Card, Card Authorization, Card Transaction Capture, Merchant Relations, Merchant Acquiring Facility, Card Network Participant Facility, Credit Card Position Keeping

### Payments
- Payment Order, Payment Execution, Payment Instruction, Payment Rail Operations, Financial Gateway, ACH Operations, Cheque Processing, Card Clearing, Card Financial Settlement, Card eCommerce Gateway, Central Cash Handling, Correspondent Bank Operations, Financial Message Analysis

### Trade Finance
- Letter of Credit, Bank Guarantee, Credit Facility, Credit Management, Project Finance, Syndicated Loan, Factoring, Limit and Exposure Management

### Treasury
- Corporate Treasury, Corporate Treasury Analysis, Asset And Liability Management, Stock Lending and Repos, Asset Securitization, Bank Portfolio Administration, Bank Portfolio Analysis

### Cash Management
- Cash Management And Account Services, Cash Concentration, Notional Pooling, Direct Debit, Direct Debit Mandate, Cheque Lock Box, Corporate Payroll Services

### Risk & Compliance
- Regulatory Reporting, Regulatory Compliance, Compliance Reporting, Guideline Compliance, Financial Accounting, Financial Statement Assessment, Fraud Evaluation, Fraud Diagnosis, Fraud Resolution, Fraud Model, Credit Risk Models, Market Risk Models, Liquidity Risk Models, Operational Risk Models

### Customer Management
- Customer Relationship Management, Party Lifecycle Management, Customer Agreement, Customer Credit Rating, Customer Behavior Insights, Customer Event History, Party Authentication, Customer Access Entitlement

### Collateral & Collections
- Collections, Collateral Allocation Management, Collateral Asset Administration, Party Asset Directory, Delinquent Account Handling, Account Recovery, Card Collections
